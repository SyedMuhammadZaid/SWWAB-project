What we do?

MISSION

SSWAB Trust Dialysis Center a major voluntary nonprofit health organization, is dedicated to preventing kidney and urinary tract diseases and early diagnosis of Diabetes & High Blood pressure, improving the health and well being of individuals and families affected by kidney disease.

VISION

To enhance the lives of everyone with, at risk of or affected by kidney disease and to ensure that no kidney patient will perish due to lack of funds

COMMITMENT

To provide high quality, FREE of cost dialysis., low cost treatment to needy kidney patients of Diabetes & High Blood pressure and chronic kidney diseases.To promote public awareness and education of kidney diseases.

About Us

We provide quality dialysis, gives more time to spend fit & healthy lifestyle.

SSWAB Trust(Kidney Care & Dialysis Centre) is serving patients who are suffering from Hypertension, Diabetes and Kidney Diseases under the supervision of renowned Nephrologist Prof. Dr Waqar H Kazmi and his professional team, since 2009. SSWAB Trust Dialysis Centre is a state of the art dialysis centre in Karachi, having the latest equipment and machinery.
